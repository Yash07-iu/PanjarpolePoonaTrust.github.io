About Us

The Pune Panjarpole Trust, established in 1855, is a sanctuary for over 2,200 cattle. Located in Bhosari, the Trust provides shelter, food, and medical care to abandoned and aging cattle. Our mission is to create a compassionate environment where every animal is treated with dignity and respect. We are committed to sustainable cattle farming and community welfare, blending tradition with modern care.

Our Mission

To create a sustainable and sacred environment for the welfare of cows and humanity. We strive to promote ethical cattle farming, educate the community, and inspire compassion for all living beings. Our trust is dedicated to providing lifelong care, medical support, and a loving home for every animal in need.
